# DriveWise AI

**Live Demo:** https://ai-smart-car-advisor-master.vercel.app/

**Youtube Pitching Video** : https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3dqfe8eYor8

DriveWise AI is an interactive financial literacy platform that helps users make **informed, responsible car purchasing decisions**. 

By simulating the true cost of ownership and providing personalized financial insights,
the system empowers users to understand how a major purchase impacts their financial wellbeing over time.

## 🔍 Background

Purchasing a car is one of the most significant financial decisions for young adults and fresh graduates.
In many ASEAN countries, including Malaysia, buyers often evaluate affordability based mainly on the **monthly loan installment**, 
overlooking key recurring expenses such as insurance, fuel, maintenance, and road tax (OECD, 2021; Investopedia, 2023).

Research shows that limited financial literacy leads many individuals to underestimate the full cost of ownership,
resulting in overcommitment and increased long-term risk (Lusardi & Mitchell, 2014; World Bank, 2022).

Existing tools typically focus on general budgeting or basic loan calculators and do not provide purchase-specific
financial guidance reflective of true ownership costs (Consumer Financial Protection Bureau, 2016).

DriveWise AI addresses this challenge by offering a comprehensive financial simulation and advisory experience tailored to vehicle ownership.

## 🚀 Features

### 📊 User Profile Input
Users enter:
- Monthly salary  
- Current savings  
- Monthly living expenses  
- Monthly financial commitments  
- Desired car price and preferred brand  
- BUDI95 eligibility status

### 📈 Affordability Analysis
- Debt-to-Income (DTI) calculation  
- Emergency fund readiness assessment  
- Financial Health Score  
- Monthly leftover and lifestyle impact meter

### 🚘 True Cost of Car Ownership
Shows total monthly and annual cost including:
- Loan installment  
- Insurance  
- Fuel  
- Maintenance  
- Road tax

### 💡 Life Impact Simulator
Visual comparison of financial outcomes over 5 years:
- Buying the selected car  
- Buying a cheaper alternative  
- Investing the difference instead

### 🏪 Dealership Locator
Displays nearby car dealerships that match the user’s recommended budget (via Google Maps Embed).

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. Enter your financial profile and desired car details.  
2. The system performs rule-based evaluations using financial best practices.  
3. Users receive personalized insights, risk indicators, and affordability scores.  
4. The Life Impact Simulator visualizes long-term consequences of different decisions.  
5. Suggested dealerships are displayed to provide real-world purchase options.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

**Frontend**
- React.js — Dynamic UI framework  
- Vite — Fast development & build tool  
- Tailwind CSS — Utility-first styling  
- Lucide Icons — UI iconography  
- Google Maps Embed API — Dealership location integration

**Architecture**
- Frontend-only web application
- Data processing simulated in-browser
- Temporary session storage via `localStorage`
- Rule-based financial logic (no backend required)

## 🎯 Why It Matters

DriveWise AI contributes to building financial resilience by helping users:
- Understand the **true affordability** of a car, not just the monthly payment
- Evaluate **financial risk** before committing to a major purchase
- Make smarter decisions rooted in **long-term financial planning**
- Avoid unnecessary debt and preserve savings

This is especially relevant in ASEAN where financial literacy has not kept pace with rapid digitization of financial services.
